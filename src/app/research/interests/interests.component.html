Research Interests

The Story of my Research Work ...

Since my second year of bachelor studies I am involved in the research area of linear programming and interior-point algorithms. During that year I was researching the solution of linear complementarity (LCP) and weighted linear complementarity problems (WLCP), through reading articles and implementing a solver application. After finishing my bachelor studies, I started to search after similar topics and develop further my application. In the year of 2022, during master studies, I participated in a conference too, organized by the Transylvanian Museum Association (eme) research group, where I presented my solver's solutions, my observations and conclusions related to WLCPs. Also now I am part of this research group and I am continuing my project in this area.

Nowadays, I am also doing research on web development and I am involved in topics like responsive and adaptive web design, web services and security. My work focuses on a systematic study on the security features provided by SOAP and RESTful Services and tries to address the different issues faced in security and presents the research scope in the area of web security.
